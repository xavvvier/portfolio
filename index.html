Javier Gonzalez

## Web developer ##

Software developer, cyclist.

Embracing the change and seeking to improve things with a positive outlook. Quick learner, adaptable, work well in teams or independently. Enthusiastic about making meaningful differences in the lives of people through software. Not daunted by large, complex applications... they just require more time.

I remember creating complex formulas in Excel back in high school when we had to build a pay sheet as a project in the accounting class. For some reason I ended up building some big formulas easily while my friends were struggling, after a while I was explaining some Excel tricks to my teacher and friends.

My interesting in programming began while taking a computer maintenance program. There was a course on how to build VB macros in MS Excel, which I sailed through and suddenly I grasped the basic concepts of logic. I dropped out of that program and started a new one focused only on programming.

I don't consider myself a smart guy. But I do think that commitment and perseverance can take you wherever you want to be.
